Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2, 2026 — compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

1. Data controller

The controller for personal data collected via Relief is the Relief team, reachable at: contact@relief-app.fr.

2. Data collected

Relief does not collect any personal data on a remote server. Information related to using the Application (answers given during guided journeys, identified emotions, completed activities, emotional overview history) is saved locally only, directly on the user's device, and never leaves it.

3. Purpose of processing

  • Local storage of data enables the Application's features to function (guided journeys, activities, emotional overview) and preserves session history from one use to the next, directly on the device.
  • Relief does not use personal data for targeted advertising purposes and does not sell it to any third party.

4. Legal basis

Processing is based on the performance of the service requested by the user. As data is stored exclusively on-device and never transmitted, it is not otherwise processed by the publisher.

5. Storage and security

Data is stored exclusively on the user's device, within the Application's own storage space. It is subject to iOS's native security mechanisms and is automatically deleted if the Application is uninstalled.

6. Data sharing

As data is stored exclusively on-device, no data is shared with any third party or transmitted to the publisher.

7. Retention period

Data is retained locally until the user deletes the relevant sessions or uninstalls the Application. Uninstalling the Application permanently deletes all stored data.

8. User rights

In accordance with the GDPR, users have the following rights over their data: access, rectification, erasure, restriction, portability, and objection. They may also set guidelines regarding the fate of their data after their death.

These rights can be exercised by writing to contact@relief-app.fr. Users also have the right to lodge a complaint with the relevant data protection authority (in France, the CNIL — www.cnil.fr).

9. Intended audience and minimum age

The Application does not require creating an account, and its use is not recommended for children under the age of 13. No data is knowingly collected from users below this age.

10. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated at any time to reflect changes to the Application or to applicable regulations. Users will be informed of any material change.
